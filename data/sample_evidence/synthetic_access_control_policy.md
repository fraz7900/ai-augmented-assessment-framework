# Access Control Policy

This policy establishes access control requirements for Northwind Grid Utility's information and operational technology (OT) systems, consistent with least-privilege and separation-of-duties principles.

# Scope

This policy applies to all personnel, contractors, and third parties with logical or physical access to Northwind Grid Utility IT systems and to OT systems supporting generation, transmission, and distribution operations.

# Account Provisioning

All user accounts require documented manager approval prior to provisioning. Access requests are reviewed against a role-based access matrix maintained by the Identity and Access Management team. Standing access to OT systems is limited to personnel with a demonstrated operational need, reviewed quarterly.

# Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication is required for all remote access to the corporate network and for all interactive access to systems classified as critical to bulk electric system reliability.

# Access Review

Access rights are reviewed on a quarterly basis by system owners. Any account inactive for more than 90 days is automatically disabled pending manager reauthorization. Access review findings are logged and retained for two years to support audit and compliance reporting.

# Third-Party Access

Third-party vendor access to OT systems requires a signed access agreement, time-bounded credentials, and session monitoring. Vendor access is revoked immediately upon contract termination or change in scope of work.

# Incident-Driven Access Suspension

Upon detection of anomalous account activity, the Security Operations Center may suspend account access pending investigation, consistent with the organization's Event and Incident Response procedures.
